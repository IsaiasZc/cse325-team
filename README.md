### Market Inventory Application

#### What problem does your application solve or what need does it meet?
The problem our app will solve is to help businesses that need to manage inventory so it can make it easier for them to track stocks in their company. By keeping track of the inventory, it will make it easier for businesses to prepare reports for their inventory, which can help them make important and decisive decisions.

#### What makes your idea interesting or valuable?
We have noticed that many inventory management systems are very expensive and small companies cannot afford them. Through our app, we will make it cheaper and more affordable—giving small businesses access to high-quality software with premium features to manage their inventory, personalized specifically for them.

#### Summary:
An application that will have the ability to check and administer the inventory of a store.
 
#### Target Audience / Users:
This app is intended for all businesses, enterprises, and individuals who deal with stocks and inventory. Through this app, they can boost the productivity of their employees, resulting in higher efficiency and larger profit margins.

#### What's IN (Features):
- **User Authentication:** Login capabilities with two types of roles: Admin and Standard User.
- **Dashboard:** A central hub to see products and stock overviews.
- **Inventory Management (CRUD):** Users can Create, Read, Update, and Delete inventory items.
- **Specialized List:** Users can create a special list of items that must be moved to another store.
- **Search Functionality:** A search bar to quickly locate inventory items.
 
#### Technical Considerations:
* **Data Storage:** We will use an SQL database server to store the information of our users and our inventories.
* **User Accounts:** Yes, the application has two types of users. The administrator will create the account and have full rights, while standard users will have limited permissions.
* **External Services:** We are not going to use any external APIs.
* **Device Compatibility:** We are going to create a responsive web app that will adapt according to the user's device, meaning it will work seamlessly on mobile, tablets, and desktops.
* **Basic Security:** We will utilize built-in authentication and authorization systems in our web app for security. This will protect the app from vulnerabilities like SQL injection and keep user data secure.
